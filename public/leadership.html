Mr. Jaynarayan Tiwari

CMD & Founder
(opens in new tab)

Mr. Jaynarayan Tiwari brings over 18 years of industry experience, having held leadership roles across top recruitment firms in India. His career began at Hindustan Unilever Ltd., followed by key positions at Randstad India Ltd., TeamLease Services Ltd., and Ikya Human Capital Solutions Pvt. Ltd. He has also contributed to Ideal Education, further honing his expertise in people management.

A natural leader with a unique blend of logic and empathy, he has successfully led training programs, talent acquisition strategies, and workforce solutions. In 2017, driven by his passion and vision, he founded Stone OnePoint Solutions Pvt. Ltd., an end-to-end HR solutions provider specializing in recruitment, payroll management, compliance, and corporate training.

Beyond his professional journey, he is a devotee of Lord Krishna and lives by the philosophy:

"Do everything you have to do, but not with ego, lust, or envy—do it with love, compassion, humility, and devotion."

Mr. Deepak Jaiswal

Director

Deepak Jaiswal, Director of Stone OnePoint Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Pvt Ltd, began his career with Randstad India Ltd, where his hard work and enthusiasm earned him 26 national-level certificates in recruitment. Later, he transitioned into his family business to support his father. Deepak is known for his excellent people judgment skills and a strong understanding of the market, which, along with his "never say never" attitude, sets him apart from others. With his extensive background in recruitment, Deepak easily connected with Stone OnePoint Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Pvt Ltd, where his expertise continues to shine.

Mr. Harshit Kadhi

CFO
(opens in new tab)

A Chartered Accountant since 2011 and a dedicated member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, Mr. Harshit Kadhi brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise across multiple areas of the profession, including Direct and Indirect Taxation, Auditing, and Advisory services. He specializes in Goods and Service Tax (GST), Maharashtra Value Added Tax (MVAT), and Service Tax. With a proven track record of successfully representing clients before various tax authorities in VAT, GST, and Income Tax matters, he has established himself as a trusted expert in the field. Mr. Harshit Kadhi is also a sought-after speaker, conducting corporate training and seminars on GST for various organizations. His deep understanding of the business environment, combined with a solid foundation in financial and accounting principles, enables him to guide firms in making critical financial and management decisions.

Mr. Pranavkumar Subramaniyan

Executive Director & Shareholder
(opens in new tab)

Pranavkumar Subramaniyan serves as the Executive Director and Shareholder at Stone OnePoint Solutions Pvt. Ltd., where he plays a pivotal role in shaping and driving the sales and operations across both Permanent and Contract staffing businesses. With an impressive 18-year career in the staffing industry, Pranav has developed deep expertise in the diverse facets of HR, staffing, payroll, and compliance. He has made significant contributions to renowned organizations such as HirePro, TeamLease, Innov, Alp Consulting, and Times of India, where he has honed his skills in delivering innovative staffing solutions and streamlining operational processes.

Pranav’s vast experience across different sectors and his ability to understand the evolving demands of the staffing industry have enabled him to create value for clients by aligning their talent acquisition strategies with their business goals. His leadership and strategic vision continue to be key drivers in the growth and success of Stone OnePoint Solutions Pvt. Ltd.. Passionate about people and process, he constantly strives to build stronger teams and deliver impactful results in the ever-changing staffing landscape.
